# E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project was completed during my **Data Analytics Internship** and focuses on analyzing an e-commerce sales dataset to identify purchasing trends, evaluate product performance, and generate actionable business insights.

Using Microsoft Excel, the dataset was cleaned, explored, and summarized through descriptive statistics, pivot tables, and time-series analysis to understand sales performance between **January 2023 and June 2025**.

---

## Dataset

The analysis is based on **1,200 customer orders** containing transactional and sales information.

### Key Attributes

* **Order Details:** Order ID, Order Date, Order Status, Tracking Number
* **Customer Information:** Customer ID, Shipping Address
* **Product Information:** Product Category, Quantity, Unit Price, Total Price, Items in Cart
* **Sales & Marketing:** Payment Method, Coupon Code, Referral Source

---

##  Project Structure

### **Sheet 1 – Raw Data & Descriptive Statistics**

* Cleaned dataset of 1,200 orders
* Descriptive statistics (Mean, Median, Average Order Value)
* Outlier identification
* Initial business observations

### **Sheet 2 – Product Performance Analysis**

* Pivot tables summarizing quantity sold by product
* Product-wise sales comparison
* Identification of top-performing products

### **Sheet 3 – Time Series Analysis**

* Daily order trend analysis
* Sales trend visualization
* Seasonal and growth pattern identification

---

## Key Insights

* **1,200** customer orders analyzed.
* **3,535** total items sold.
* **Average Order Value:** **$1,053.97**
* **Median Order Value:** **$823.62**, indicating the presence of high-value outliers.
* Customers purchased an average of **3 items per order**.
* **Chairs** and **Printers** recorded the highest sales volumes.
* **Instagram** and **Email** were the most effective referral sources.
* **Online Payments** and **Cash** were the most frequently used payment methods.
